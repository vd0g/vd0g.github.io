The point of interest here is space, and how you can become an amateur astronomer! This is a hobby that many people participate in, whether it is alone or in groups. You can spend your time observing celestial objects from space right in your backyard. By making an inital investment and purchasing or borrowing a telescope, you can be well on your way to experiencing everything the cosmos has to offer.

The internet provies much information out there, so spend your time resarching different ways, and find the best one for you. The purpose here is to raise interest, and get you started. There are many different methods and techniques to approaching this. Many of which are listed in this wikipedia page: Wikipedia Amateur Astronomy
